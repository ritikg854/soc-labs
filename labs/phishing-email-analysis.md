# Phishing Email Analysis Lab

## Objective

The objective of this lab is to practice reviewing suspicious email characteristics and documenting the types of indicators that may suggest phishing or other malicious intent.

## Scenario

In this lab, I reviewed the structure and content of a suspicious email to identify potential phishing indicators. The focus was on analyst-style observation, triage thinking, and documentation rather than deep malware analysis.

## Key Areas Reviewed

- Sender address and domain
- Subject line and urgency cues
- Suspicious links or mismatched URLs
- Attachment-related risk indicators
- Language, tone, and social engineering tactics
- Signs of impersonation or brand misuse

## What I Practiced

- Reviewing common phishing characteristics
- Identifying social engineering red flags
- Assessing suspicious email elements in a structured way
- Distinguishing normal communication from potentially malicious messaging
- Writing investigation-style notes clearly

## Outcome

This lab helped strengthen my understanding of phishing triage and the practical review steps that support SOC operations and incident response workflows.
